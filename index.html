Bootcamp 2021: Geometric Animations

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of variables, loops, and randomness. I created a geometric animation art piece that overlaps and blends the colourss that overlap with each to create a smooth blend between the different colours produced randomly. i started with a rectangle and cirlce and used loops and variables to make a grid of shapes covering the sketch. I used different colours and shapes to create a visually pleasing and inetresting grid of shapes with a bouncing ball in the background.
